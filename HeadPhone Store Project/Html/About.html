About Us

Our mission is to help more people experience great sound.

SDHP.com has been around since 1995. Long before the iPod and mobile revolution that saw headphones become one of the 3-4 items that people carried everywhere they went.

Over the past 28 years, a lot has changed at headphone.com but the common thread is a love of great sound and the joy of helping people experience it.
